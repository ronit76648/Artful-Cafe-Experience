Add 7 Balance to your home screen — works like an app, no App Store needed.

In Chrome, tap the menu and choose "Add to Home Screen":

1 Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top-right of Chrome
2 Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
3 Tap "Add" to confirm — the icon appears on your home screen

Open this page in Safari (not Chrome) and follow these steps:

1 Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari
2 Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3 Tap "Add" in the top-right — done!

In Chrome or Edge, install from the address bar:

1 Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar — top right
2 Click it and then click "Install"
3 The app opens in its own window and appears in your Start menu / Dock
